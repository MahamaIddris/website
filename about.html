WELCOME TO BORENYI ACADEMY

At Borenyi, we believe in fostering a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world.

Early Childhood Education:

we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to excel. Our school is more than just a place of learning; it's a warm and welcoming community where children are encouraged to explore, discover, and grow in a safe and nurturing environment.

Holistic Development:

Founded in April 2023, Borenyi Academy has been a cornerstone of the community, serving students from diverse backgrounds and cultures. Our inclusive atmosphere promotes acceptance, respect, and understanding among all members of our school family.

Parental Involvement:

We believe that parents are essential partners in a child's education. We encourage open communication and collaboration between parents, teachers, and the school community. Through regular updates, parent-teacher conferences, and volunteer opportunities, we strive to keep parents informed and involved in their child's learning journey.

Qualified and Experienced Professionals:

At Borenyi, teaching is more than just a job—it’s a calling. Our educators are deeply passionate about inspiring young minds and fostering a love for learning. They are dedicated to creating engaging lessons that spark curiosity, encourage exploration, and ignite a lifelong passion for knowledge.

Academic Excellence:

We strive for academic excellence by offering a comprehensive curriculum that challenges and inspires students to reach their full potential. Our dynamic teachers utilize innovative teaching methods and technologies to engage students in their learning journey.

Community Engagement:

At Borenyi Academy, we recognize the importance of nurturing the whole child. In addition to academics, we provide opportunities for students to explore their interests and talents through extracurricular activities, sports, and the arts. We believe in cultivating well-rounded individuals who are prepared to make positive contributions to society.

We understand the importance of fostering strong partnerships between the school, parents, and the community. Through open communication and collaboration, we work together to support student success and create a supportive learning environment.

Admission Process:

  1. Fill Registration Form: Begin by completing Registration form. Provide basic information about your child and family, along with any relevant documents such as birth cert and previous school records.
  2. Visit Our Campus: We invite you and your child to visit our campus for a tour and to meet our facolty. This is an opportunity for you to experience our learning environment firsthand and ask any questions you may have.
  3. Parent Interview: We value open communication with our families. During the parent interview, we’ll discuss your child’s educational goals, interests, and any specific needs they may have. This conversation helps us tailor our program to meet your child’s unique needs.
  4. Student Assessment: As part of the admissions process, your child will participate in a developmental assessment. This informal evaluation helps us understand their current skills and abilities and ensures that they are placed in the appropriate grade level.
  5. Admission Decision: After completing the application process, you will receive an admission decision. If accepted, we will provide you with all the necessary information to complete the enrollment process and prepare for your child’s first day at Borenyi.

Tuition and Financial Aid:

We believe that every child deserves access to a quality education. Our tuition fees are competitive, and we offer financial aid opportunities to eligible families by waiving tuition fee of a third ward if you have three or more wards attending Borenyi. We are committed to working with families to make our program accessible to all students.

Thank you for considering Borenyi Academy for your child's education. We look forward to welcoming you to our school community!
